Short Bio
In 2018, I was a junior Simons fellow, awarded by the Simons Society of Fellows, at Cornell Tech, hosted by Rafael Pass. In 2017, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Computer Science Department at Cornell University, hosted by Rafael Pass, Elaine Shi and Muthu Venkitasubramaniam . In December 2016, I completed my Ph.D. at Århus University in the Crypto Group, under the supervision of Prof. Ivan Damgård. I received my M.Sc. in Mathematics of Cryptography and Communications from Royal Holloway University of London in 2012, under the supervision of Prof. Kenneth G. Paterson. Prior to that, in 2011, I received my B.Sc. in Computer Science and Economics from University of Macedonia in Greece, under the supervision of Prof. George Stephanides.
